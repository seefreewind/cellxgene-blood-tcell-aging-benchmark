# ISCLS Journal Compliance Checklist

Target journal: Interdisciplinary Sciences: Computational Life Sciences.

## Checked Items

- Article type framed as Original Research Article.
- Separate blinded main manuscript and title page are provided.
- Main manuscript is anonymized for double-blind review.
- Abstract is structured and citation-free.
- Keywords are provided.
- Main text avoids clinical aging-clock, mechanism-discovery, pan-tissue, and all-foundation-model claims.
- Declarations are located on the title page, not in the blinded main manuscript.
- Public repository and Zenodo DOI are included only in non-blinded files.

## Manual Checks Before Upload

- Confirm Springer/ISCLS upload categories for supplementary files.
- Confirm whether the editorial office allows repository/DOI disclosure on the separate title page during double-blind review.
- Verify funding and author-contribution wording with all authors.
- Confirm local institutional policy for secondary public-data analysis.
